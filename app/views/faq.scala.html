Q. How Smart is the Pool?

Although we are just launching, our current pool consists of over 1000 students from institutions such as IIT, IIM, BITS and many more. These students have studied a range of disciplines; have diverse work experiences, and unique skillsets. We are not limited to any degree or diploma type, meaning PHDs and Bachelors students can participate. They want to build the skills needed for the jobs they want, and they want to see a prosperous India in the years to come.
